About us

The Location

Based in the carpark at Red Rocks, Te Kopahou Reserve. Carabean has been up and running since 2019. This beautiful but rugged spot mesmerises people on a daily basis. From the ocean to the hills to the views of the south island, this area is forever changing on a daily basis.

The
Caravan

As the caravan is located on Wellington’s rugged south coast, opening hours are occasionally affected by weather conditions. During periods of severe wind or heavy rain, Carabean may need to close for safety reasons.

We offer a range of HOT and COLD drinks, including coffee, tea, sodas, hot chocolates, iced coffees, and iced chocolates, alongside a selection of both savoury and sweet baking options.

Any unexpected closures or updates will be posted on our Facebook page.

We look forward to welcoming you to Carabean and sharing this beautiful corner of Red Rocks with you.
